NATURE OF BUSINESS, CORPORATE STRUCTURE, AND DEFINITIONS

Bencal Corporation

Province of British Columbia, Canada

Reference Date: 2026-05-29

Bencal Corporation is a British Columbia corporation that organises, capitalises, and administers private capital vehicles for accredited investors. This insert is prepared by counsel for Bencal Corporation and may be incorporated by reference into agreements to which Bencal Corporation or any Bencal Group entity is a party. This document sets out the corporate structure of the Bencal Group, the nature of its business, and the defined terms applicable to such agreements.

The Bencal Group operates through the following corporate platform:

Entity Role Code

Bencal Investment Management Inc. acts as general partner, promoter, and manager for each investment vehicle organised by Bencal Corporation, holds required securities registrations, and employs personnel. Bencal Corporation holds the equity interests in Bencal Investment Management Inc. and in each strategy-specific general partner corporation.

3. Investment Strategy Vehicles (Club Deals)

Bencal Corporation organises club deal investment vehicles in three strategy families. Each deal is structured as a separate numbered general partner corporation and limited partnership, with [N] denoting the sequential deal number:

Strategy General Partner Corporation Limited Partnership Scope
Credit Bencal Credit Partners [N] Inc. Bencal Credit Partners [N] Limited Partnership Private debt, mezzanine, senior secured loans, structured income
Real Assets Bencal Real Assets [N] Inc. Bencal Real Assets [N] Limited Partnership Real estate, infrastructure, tangible-asset direct investments
Equity Bencal Equity Partners [N] Inc. Bencal Equity Partners [N] Limited Partnership Growth equity, co-investment, direct equity participations

5. Nature of Business

Bencal Corporation is a Canadian private capital manager that organises and promotes investment vehicles for accredited investors pursuant to applicable prospectus exemptions under NI 45-106. Investment vehicles are structured on a deal-by-deal basis; investors subscribe to identified transactions in advance of any commitment. Bencal Investment Management Inc. holds a co-investment interest in each vehicle, aligning principal and investor interests.

Bencal Corporation currently operates as a promoter relying on the accredited investor prospectus exemption under section 2.3 of NI 45-106. Securities of Bencal Group vehicles are distributed through a referral arrangement with Altas One Digital Securities Inc., a registered exempt market dealer. It is intended that Bencal Investment Management Inc. will seek registration as an exempt market dealer, and subsequently as a portfolio manager and investment fund manager, as assets under management expand.

No offering document in respect of any Bencal Group vehicle has been filed with any securities regulatory authority; all distributions are made in reliance on applicable prospectus exemptions.

6. Definitions

In any agreement into which this insert is incorporated by reference, the following terms shall have the meanings set out below:

“Accredited Investor” means an individual or entity that qualifies as an “accredited investor” within the meaning of section 1.1 of NI 45-106.

“Affiliate”, when used to indicate a relationship with a specified Person, means another Person that directly, or indirectly through one or more intermediaries or otherwise, controls, or is controlled by, or is under common control with, such specified Person. A corporation shall be deemed to be an Affiliate of another corporation if one of them is the Subsidiary of the other or if both are Subsidiaries of the same Person or if each of them is directly or indirectly controlled by the same Person.

“Agent” means Altas One Digital Securities Inc., a registered exempt market dealer, acting as referral agent pursuant to the Referral Agreement.

“Bencal Corporation” means Bencal Corporation, a company incorporated under the Business Corporations Act (British Columbia), acting as parent holding company of the Bencal Group.

“Bencal Group” means Bencal Corporation together with Bencal Investment Management Inc. and all general partner corporations and limited partnerships in which Bencal Corporation holds a direct or indirect interest.

“Bencal Management” means Bencal Investment Management Inc. (BCL-CA-01-OPR), the operating manager and licensed manager of the Bencal Group, formerly known as Bencal Management Corp.

“Bencal SPV1” means Bencal Special Purpose 1 Inc. (BCL-CA-01-AST), a private investment vehicle of the Bencal Group.

“Bencal SPV2” means, collectively, Bencal Special Purpose 2 Inc. (BCL-CA-02-ADM) and Bencal Special Purpose 2 Limited Partnership (BCL-CA-02-AST).

“Bencal SPV2-GP” means Bencal Special Purpose 2 Inc. (BCL-CA-02-ADM), in its capacity as general partner of Bencal SPV2-LP.

“Bencal SPV2-LP” means Bencal Special Purpose 2 Limited Partnership (BCL-CA-02-AST).

“BenCo” means BenCo Inc.

“Club Deal” means a private placement of limited partnership interests in a numbered Bencal vehicle to a defined group of Accredited Investors in respect of an identified investment transaction.

“General Partner” means, in respect of any Bencal limited partnership, the general partner corporation of that limited partnership as identified in the applicable limited partnership agreement.

“Kiel Capital” means Kiel Capital Inc. (BCL-CA-01-HLD), a personal holding company of a Principal.

“Limited Partnership” means a limited partnership formed under the Partnership Act (British Columbia) or applicable provincial partnership legislation.

“NI 31-103” means National Instrument 31-103 Registration Requirements, Exemptions and Ongoing Registrant Obligations, as amended from time to time.

“NI 45-106” means National Instrument 45-106 Prospectus Exemptions, as amended from time to time.

“Professional Centres Canada LP” means Woodfine Professional Centres Limited Partnership.

“Professional Centres Mexico FIBRA” means Woodfine Professional Centres 4 Fideicomiso de Infraestructura en Bienes Raíces.

“Professional Centres Spain SOCIMI” means Woodfine Professional Centres 3 Sociedad Anónima Cotizada de Inversión Inmobiliaria.

“Professional Centres United States LP” means Woodfine Professional Centres 2 Limited Partnership.

“Promoter” has the meaning ascribed to it under NI 45-106.

“Prospectus Exemption” means the exemption from the prospectus requirement available under NI 45-106, including the accredited investor exemption under section 2.3 thereof, or such other exemption as may be relied upon from time to time.

“Woodfine Group” means Woodfine Capital Projects Inc. together with its Affiliates, including each Woodfine Professional Centres entity and any general partner, administrator, or special purpose vehicle related thereto.
