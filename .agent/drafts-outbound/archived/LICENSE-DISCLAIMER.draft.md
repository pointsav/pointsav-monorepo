# Legal Disclaimer — Woodfine Location Intelligence

The Woodfine Location Intelligence platform (`gis.woodfinegroup.com`) provides synthesized location intelligence metrics "as is." 

## 1. Metric Synthesis
Scores, ranks, and spend estimates are the result of computational modeling using various open-source inputs. These metrics are designed for regional market analysis and trend identification, not for high-precision measurement.

## 2. No Guarantee
While we strive for high fidelity by combining multiple authoritative sources, Woodfine Group does not guarantee the precision of specific retailer coordinates or synthesized economic metrics. Information provided on the map is for informational and research purposes only.

## 3. Privacy & Ethics
No individual-level tracking or personally identifiable information (PII) is utilized in the generation of our mobility or demographic layers. All mobility data is derived from synthetic trajectories (WorldMove) or anonymized, aggregated visitor patterns.

## 4. Usage Restrictions
This platform and its data are not intended for:
*   Real-time navigation or logistics routing.
*   Critical infrastructure planning or emergency response.
*   High-stakes site selection without independent field verification.

## 5. Non-Endorsement
Reference to specific commercial brands, chains, or logos does not constitute an endorsement by those entities of Woodfine Group, nor an endorsement by Woodfine Group of those entities.
