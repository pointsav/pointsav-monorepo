Bencal Corporation

Bencal Corporation is a British Columbia corporation that organises, capitalises, and administers private capital vehicles for accredited investors. This document is prepared by Management of Bencal Corporation and may be incorporated by reference into agreements to which Bencal Corporation or any Bencal Group entity is a party. This document sets out the corporate structure of the Bencal Group, the nature of its business, and the defined terms applicable to such agreements.

5. Nature of Business

Bencal Corporation is a Canadian private capital manager that organises and promotes investment vehicles for accredited investors pursuant to applicable prospectus exemptions under NI 45-106. Investment vehicles are structured on a deal-by-deal basis; investors subscribe to identified transactions in advance of any commitment. Bencal Corporation takes its compensation primarily in the form of unit-based or share-based interests in each vehicle, aligning the interests of the principal shareholders with those of investors.

Bencal Corporation currently operates as a promoter relying on the accredited investor prospectus exemption under section 2.3 of NI 45-106. Securities of Bencal Group vehicles are distributed through a referral arrangement with Altas One Digital Securities Inc., a registered exempt market dealer. It is intended that the proposed Bencal Investment Management Inc. will seek registration as an exempt market dealer to formalise the distribution of Bencal Group vehicles as assets under management expand.

No offering documents in respect of any Bencal Group vehicles have been filed with any securities regulatory authority; all distributions are made in reliance on applicable prospectus exemptions.

6. Definitions

In any agreement into which this insert is incorporated by reference, the following terms shall have the meanings set out below:

“Accredited Investor” means an individual or entity that qualifies as an “accredited investor” within the meaning of section 1.1 of NI 45-106.

“Affiliate”, when used to indicate a relationship with a specified Person, means another Person that directly, or indirectly through one or more intermediaries or otherwise, controls, or is controlled by, or is under common control with, such specified Person. A corporation shall be deemed to be an Affiliate of another corporation if one of them is the Subsidiary of the other or if both are Subsidiaries of the same Person or if each of them is directly or indirectly controlled by the same Person.

“Agent” means Altas One Digital Securities Inc., a registered exempt market dealer, acting as referral agent pursuant to the Referral Agreement.

“Bencal Corporation” means Bencal Corporation, a company incorporated under the Business Corporations Act (British Columbia), acting as parent holding company of the Bencal Group.

“Bencal Equity Partners 1” means Bencal Equity Partners 1 Inc. (BCL-CA-01-AST), a private investment vehicle of the Bencal Group.

“Bencal Group” means Bencal Corporation together with Bencal Investment Management Inc. and all general partner corporations and limited partnerships in which Bencal Corporation holds a direct or indirect interest.

“Bencal Management” means Bencal Investment Management Inc. (BCL-CA-01-OPR), the operating manager and licensed manager of the Bencal Group.

“Bencal Real Assets 1” means, collectively, Bencal Real Assets 1 Inc. (BCL-CA-01-ADM) and Bencal Real Assets 1 Limited Partnership (BCL-CA-01-AST).

“Bencal Real Assets 1 LP” means Bencal Real Assets 1 Limited Partnership (BCL-CA-01-AST).
