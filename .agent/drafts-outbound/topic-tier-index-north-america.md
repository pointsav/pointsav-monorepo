# North American Co-location Tier Index

The North American Location Intelligence index evaluates the commercial convergence of major retail operators across the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Utilizing the [Retail Co-location Methodology](topic-co-location-methodology.md), the index identifies the highest-value commercial nodes based on the presence of Primary Targets (Walmart Supercentres) and their proximity to Secondary (Costco, Home Depot) and Tertiary (Healthcare, Higher Education) anchors.

## Market Scarcity and Tier 5 Validation

Achieving a **★★★★★ Tier 5** designation requires the simultaneous convergence of four independent, capital-intensive operators (Primary + Two Secondaries + Two Tertiaries) within a tight geographic radius. Because of these stringent criteria, Tier 5 sites represent a fraction of the total North American retail landscape. They are the apex of commercial validation, analogous to a 3-star Michelin rating for culinary excellence—indicating a market node "worth a special journey" for capital deployment.
