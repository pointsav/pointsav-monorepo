# Co-location Index: Canada

The Canadian Co-location Index is a subset of the [North American Top 400 Index](topic-tier-index-north-america.md). It identifies the high-value commercial nodes within the Canadian market that meet the stringent linear-geometry requirements of the Woodfine [Leapfrog 2030 Methodology](topic-co-location-methodology.md).

## Market Overview
The Canadian market is characterized by a high concentration of Alpha Anchors (Walmart Canada, Real Canadian Superstore) in key urban corridors. The index focuses on the spatial convergence of these anchors with secondary hardware (Home Depot/Canadian Tire) and warehouse (Costco) infrastructure.
