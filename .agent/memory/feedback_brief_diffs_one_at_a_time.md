When presenting BRIEF conflicts or proposed edits, show one diff at a time and wait for explicit approval before applying. Do not batch-apply changes. Proposed wording for BRIEF edits must be quoted verbatim and approved before the edit tool runs.

**Why:** Operator explicitly asked for diffs "one by one with a recommendation" during the 2026-05-23 BRIEF consolidation session.

**How to apply:** Any time you are about to edit a BRIEF, convention, or DOCTRINE file based on a consolidation audit or conflict resolution — present the exact change first, get a yes, then edit.
