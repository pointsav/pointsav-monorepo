Pre-compiled, signed OS images for property owners and building operators. PointSav is open source — clone and compile from github.com/pointsav/pointsav-monorepo at no cost. Pre-compiled binaries are available here for a one-time payment in Polygon USDC: Apache 2.0 licence — $1.00 USDC or FSL licence — $19.00 USDC. No subscription, no account, no automatic updates. Each version is purchased independently.

Payment and licence key issuance are on the Licensing page. The licence key issued on payment is your authentication credential for the app-privategit-source registry, which serves additional app and service packages. No tax collected — PointSav operates below the CA $30,000 GST small-supplier threshold.
