Private Office
A separate use type · opens to shared corridor

Private Office is distinct from Professional Office. Three sizes (PO-1 / PO-2 / PO-3); no bilateral corridor — each office opens directly onto the shared building corridor.

Same zone vocabulary as Professional Office, but with no internal corridor. Habitat marginally under 6.0 m.
