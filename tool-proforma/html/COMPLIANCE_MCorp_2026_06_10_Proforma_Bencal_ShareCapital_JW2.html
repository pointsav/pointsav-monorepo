Purpose

This share capital proforma is prepared to determine the fair market value of strategic partner shareholdings in Bencal Management Corp. The primary purpose is to enable Bencal Management to assess, on a reasoned and documented basis, what portion of strategic partner shareholdings may be offered as bonus shares to initial investors in Bencal SPV1 or Bencal SPVX. All valuations are prepared on an IFRS 10.27 investment-entity basis (fair value through profit or loss) consistent with Bencal Management's accounting treatment of its investment portfolio. Strategic Partner shares that are not allocated or distributed will be retained by Keil Capital and Elzen Holdings.

The secondary purpose of this proforma is to model a share capital structure sufficient to comply with TSX Venture Exchange listing requirements, drawing on the current and planned assets of Bencal SPV1, Bencal SPV2, and the revenue arising under the WCP Allocation and Sales Agreement. Where the current structure falls short of exchange requirements on revenue or asset thresholds, this document identifies the gap and proposes structural measures — including potential adjustment to the authorized share count — to meet those requirements at the earliest practicable date. Compliance projections are forward-looking and carry standard BCSC NI 51-102 cautionary language.
