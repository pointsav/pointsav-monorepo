Purpose

This share capital proforma is prepared to determine the fair market value of strategic partner shareholdings in Bencal Management Corp. The primary purpose is to enable Bencal Management to assess, on a reasoned and documented basis, what portion of strategic partner shareholdings may be offered as bonus shares to initial investors in Bencal SPV-1 or Bencal SPVs. All valuations are prepared on an IFRS 10.27 investment-entity basis (fair value through profit or loss) consistent with Bencal Management's accounting treatment of its investment portfolio. Strategic Partner shares that are not allocated or distributed will be retained by Keil Capital and Elzen Holdings.

The secondary purpose of this proforma is to model a share capital structure sufficient to comply with TSX Venture Exchange listing requirements, drawing on the current and planned assets of Bencal SPV-1, Bencal SPV-2, and the revenue arising under the WCP Allocation and Sales Agreement. Where the current structure falls short of exchange requirements on revenue or asset thresholds, this document identifies the gap and proposes structural measures — including potential adjustment to the authorized share count — to meet those requirements at the earliest practicable date. Compliance projections are forward-looking and carry standard BCSC NI 51-102 cautionary language.

Valuation — Four Methods and Indicated Fair Value

Primary valuation method: book value / NAV per share, applied on an IFRS 10.27 FVTPL investment-entity basis (portfolio carried at fair value through profit or loss; NAV per share = IFRS book equity per share). Earnings yield method: portfolio FVTPL income per share capitalised at a 4.5% target yield — included for comparability; mechanically high in FVTPL remeasurement years and N/M in loss years; do not use as primary basis. P/E valuation: FVTPL income per share × 10.72 (blended private-market P/E drawn from comparable asset-management and investment-holding structures). NAV at −20% discount: applies a 10–30% typical holding-company discount for illiquid Level-3 minority interests; −20% is the base-case selected. Indicated FMV = simple unweighted average of applicable methods per year.

Capital structure note: All founding shares were issued at $0.0001/share, below the TSXV $0.10 minimum listing price. Shares issued below the minimum listing price are classified as escrow securities under TSXV Policy 5.4. These shares are subject to a hold period of approximately 18 to 36 months from the listing date, during which they may not be freely traded. Release is staged under a schedule agreed with the Exchange at the time of listing — typically in equal tranches (often approximately 20% of the escrowed shares per release period, over four to five periods). No share consolidation is required solely on account of the issue price; the TSXV hold period mechanism governs resale restrictions. Strategic Partner shares not yet allocated will be retained by Keil Capital and Elzen Holdings.
