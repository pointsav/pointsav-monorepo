This document contains forward-looking information, including planned net asset values, projected distributions, fair market value estimates, and share-capital scenarios. These projections are based on the FVTPL proforma prepared under IFRS 10.27 using planned/intended portfolio returns. Actual results may differ materially.

Valuation basis. NAV per share is the primary valuation method, applied consistently with IFRS 10.27 investment entity accounting (all holdings carried at fair value through profit or loss). Earnings yield and holding-company-discount methods are included for comparative purposes only; neither method is a primary basis of value. FVTPL remeasurement gains are recognised in the income statement and create mechanically high earnings-yield readings in favourable years — read alongside the NAV primary method.

Primary issuance. If Bencal Management Corp. were to issue new shares in a primary market transaction, the proceeds indicated are gross amounts. Issuance costs would be deducted directly from equity under IAS 32.37 and are not reflected in the incremental return scenarios above.
