How We Select Contributors

No resume review. No coding test. No interview. The calls and the essay are the entire process — and they select for genuine understanding, not credential theatre.
The process
Four to six conference calls
One session per categoryYou write on the slides by handQuestions drive the call
A six-to-ten page essay
Written after the callsIn your own wordsReveals real engagement
Why it works
✲When candidates feed these slides to an AI, we receive the same essay from every person who does. It is immediately identifiable. What engaged contributors produce is always genuinely different.
✲The handwritten notes on the slides are the record of a real conversation — they cannot be generated, only earned.
✲For investors, this is the signal that matters: this is how the team is built — for understanding, not for paperwork.
What You Walk Away With
At the end of 6 calls, the Contributor produces the White Paper — a scoped document defining the contribution domain, access level, and engagement terms.
