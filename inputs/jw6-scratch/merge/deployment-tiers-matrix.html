✶ A single archive is free in every model. The charge attaches to cross-archive orchestration — consolidating many buildings or entities — not to the hardware underneath.
✶ The SaaS / PaaS / IaaS split follows the standard service-model definitions (NIST SP 800-145), so a developer and a procurement officer read the same words.
✶ Models are migration paths: move a portfolio on-prem, or run several at once — a right codified in the EU Data Act (Reg. 2023/2854), Ch. VI. A planned seL4-based isolation tier is intended to harden the on-prem path further.
