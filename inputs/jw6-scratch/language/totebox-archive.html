Design System — Record Keeping

Totebox Archive

Each archive template holds the records of one entity type — Personnel, Corporate, or Real Property — so ownership transfers cleanly when a person leaves or an asset is sold.
Totebox Archive Personnel
vs.
Totebox Archive Corporate
vs.
Totebox Archive Real Property
Personnel stay separate from companies — people come and go over time.
Corporate entities stay separate because they can:
Buy a company or Buy a building or Both — company & building
Real Property is the building or physical asset — held as its own archive.
