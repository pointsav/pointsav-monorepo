The rule
✲No one reads or writes data directly. Every interaction routes through an instance of Console OS, optionally by way of Interface OS.
✲The Totebox Archive records access through the Registry Token, mapping each user device IP address to its paired permissions.
✲An explicit registry of who can do what, auditable by construction — it answers "who changed this and why?" from the record itself.
