Open-Core Model

Open-Source Base, Paid Coordination

A single archive runs free; coordinating many archives is the paid boundary, which is the exact line a buyer must price before adopting.
Free and Open Source — Base Nodes
ToteboxOS — one WORM flat-file archive per entity
ToteboxOSConsoleOSDesign SystemDocumentation
An SMB runs a complete single-entity deployment at no licence cost — the base node is the open invitation to contribute.
Proprietary — Coordination Tiers
✲OrchestrationOS — stateless cross-archive aggregation; the paid boundary begins where one archive becomes many.
✲InfrastructureOS / NetworkAdminOS — leased and hybrid deployment plus machine-based authorization across the topology.
✲Open-source-core with priced multi-archive features follows established open-core practice — Source: Open-core model (GitLab, Grafana).
