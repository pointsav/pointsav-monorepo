Where the boundary sits
✲A single archive is free in every tier. The charge attaches to cross-archive orchestration, not to the node — the open-core line stays the same regardless of who owns the hardware.
✲The SaaS / PaaS / IaaS split follows the standard service-model definitions — per NIST SP 800-145 — so procurement and audit teams map it to language they already use.
✲Tiers are migration paths, not silos: a customer may move on-prem or run several providers at once, a right codified in the EU Data Act (Reg. 2023/2854), Ch. VI.
✲For investors this is the land-and-expand surface: enter Self-Hosted or Managed, expand into orchestration. SMB net revenue retention runs ~97% — per 2024 SaaS Benchmarks (High Alpha / OpenView) — so expansion is earned, not assumed.
