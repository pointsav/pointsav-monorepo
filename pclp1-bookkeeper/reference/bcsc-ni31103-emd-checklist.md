## Registration Category: Exempt Market Dealer (EMD)

Applicable when LP units are distributed under exempt market exemptions
(Accredited Investor — NI 45-106 s.2.3; Offering Memorandum — NI 45-106 s.2.9).

---

## Must-Have Checklist

### Firm Registration

| # | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 1 | Form 33-109F4 filed via National Registration Database (NRD) |
| 2 | Ultimate Designated Person (UDP) — senior officer accountable for compliance culture |
| 3 | Chief Compliance Officer (CCO) — separately registered individual |
| 4 | Minimum working capital: **$50,000** net assets above liabilities at all times |
| 5 | Fidelity bond / insurance coverage |
| 6 | Written policies & procedures manual (compliance system) |
| 7 | BC business presence |

### Each Dealing Representative

| # | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 1 | Individual Form 33-109F4 |
| 2 | Proficiency — EMD Dealing Representative exam (or recognized equivalent) |
| 3 | Integrity/solvency assessment (criminal, credit, regulatory history) |
| 4 | Sponsorship by a registered firm |

### Ongoing Obligations (once registered)

| Obligation | NI 31-103 Reference |
|---|---|
| Know Your Client (KYC) | s.13.2 |
| Know Your Product (KYP) | s.13.2.1 |

## Issuer Exemption — May Apply to PCLP 1

Under **BC Securities Act s.34(2)(a)** and **NI 45-106 s.3.9**:

- **Issuer distributing its own securities** → potentially exempt from dealer registration
- If PCLP 1 (or its GP, Woodfine Management Corp., acting on behalf of the LP) distributes
  units directly to investors → dealer registration **may not be required**
- If a **separate entity is paid** to act as placement agent or finder → **EMD registration required**

**BCSC applies this exemption narrowly** — "acting in the capacity of the issuer" must be
genuine, not a structure designed to avoid registration. See BCSC Staff Notice 33-705.

---

## BC-Specific Notes

- Registration filed through National Registration Database (NRD)
- If Offering Memorandum used (NI 45-106 s.2.9): OM must be filed with BCSC within
  **10 days** of first distribution under it
- BCSC may impose additional terms and conditions on registration
