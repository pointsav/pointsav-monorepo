# Freely Transferable Private Equity

Multi-generational capital requires structural control and direct ownership. Our Direct-Hold Solutions are not investment funds; they operate as the enterprise itself. Administered through dedicated Woodfine subsidiaries, these vehicles provide a definitive financial model for the procurement, development, and management of institutional-grade regional real estate.

This system functions strictly as a narrow bank. We do not utilize commercial construction loans. The initial assets are built entirely with perpetual equity. Once lease cash flow stabilizes, the vehicles self-issue Exchange-Traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures to fund subsequent growth. Debt is strictly capped by a mandatory 1.2 Interest Coverage Ratio, funding up to 100% of development costs.

By taking our compensation entirely as Retained Equity—a 10% ownership stake—we eliminate variable performance fees at the enterprise level. Operating as Regulated Reporting Entities rather than layered funds creates a highly efficient capital structure. This allows independent Asset Managers to originate tailored Club Deals, Private SPVs, or local Access Funds. They can apply their own fee models while delivering exact jurisdictional alignment and custodial control for their investors.

These vehicles fund a specific Qualified Investment: the Co-Location Mandate. We target regional markets because they exhibit stable, non-cyclical demographic demand for essential services, yet suffer from a structural shortfall in institutional-grade construction. We procure, develop, and manage professional office buildings directly adjacent to major national retailers, such as Walmart, Costco, and Home Depot. This captures the resilience of daily retail foot traffic to support medical, academic, government, and professional tenants—sectors inherently resistant to Work From Home (WFH) and AI disruption because they require physical, in-person operations.
