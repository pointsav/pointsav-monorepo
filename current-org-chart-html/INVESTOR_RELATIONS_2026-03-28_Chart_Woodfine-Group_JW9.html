CORPORATE STRUCTURE

WOODFINE GROUP

Woodfine Capital Projects, a Canadian corporation, is structured to offer for sale investment units across four distinct direct-hold solutions. While the Canadian limited partnership presently exists, the remaining three investment vehicles are scheduled to be formed within the United States, Spain, and Mexico.

Woodfine Capital Projects acts as the promoter for the entire suite of solutions. Although established as closed-end investment vehicles with public, non-traded, and freely tradable units, the direct-hold solutions are engineered to meet all ongoing securities reporting requirements across their respective jurisdictions.

Provides management services to all entities.

Acts as the General Partner or Administrator for the direct-hold solutions by way of 100% subsidiaries.

Fixed annual contribution to overhead: 1% of the aggregated subscription amount, adjusted for inflation, and paid by the respective direct-hold solutions.

No NAV fees charged to the direct-hold solutions.
