TRANSACTION #3

ALLOCATION AND SALES AGREEMENTS

Woodfine Capital Projects, a Canadian corporation, is structured to offer for sale investment units across four distinct direct-hold solutions. While the Canadian limited partnership presently exists, the remaining three investment vehicles are scheduled for formation in the United States, Spain, and Mexico.

Woodfine Capital Projects acts as the promoter for the entire suite of solutions. Although established as closed-end investment vehicles with public, non-traded, and freely tradable units, the direct-hold solutions are engineered to meet all ongoing securities reporting requirements across their respective jurisdictions.

Direct-Hold Solutions

In consideration of the subscription related to the Right of First Refusal (ROFR) for each direct-hold solution (Transaction 2), Allocation and Sales Agreements will be established for Investment Units of up to:

  • CAD 75M for Canada
  • USD 150M for US
  • EUR 75M for Spain
  • MXN 1,500M for Mexico

Subscriptions of up to 10% of the GFV.

Commissions of 6% on the settlement amount.

WCP Equity Financing — pre-IPO / Direct Listing

  • CAD 12.6M or 30% of the equity financing

Representing 21% of WCP’s fully diluted share capital, based on CAD 42M in gross subscriptions.
