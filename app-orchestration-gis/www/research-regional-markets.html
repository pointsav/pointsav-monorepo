§4  ·  Discovery Score (v0.3 — composition + isolation)

Regional Market Score

score = quality × isolation × civic × confidence
quality = (1 + 0.30·√(depth−floor)) × tightness
floor = 2 if anchor_d ≤ 40 km, else 4
isolation = 0.65 + 0.35 · min(anchor_d, 90)/90
anchor_d = km to nearest T1 hub outside this market
Isolation + composition balance
Fort Wayne IN isolated 107 km · 1T1+3T2 · civic #1
Sherwood Park AB 9 km · 1T1+2T2 · civic · T1.b correction #57
Plano TX 10 km · 3T1+2T2 · deep composition #6
Mississauga ON 11 km · 2T1+1T2 · civic #7
norm_score = (score − country_min) / (country_max − country_min)
depth = (T1×4)+(T2×2)+(T3×1); floor = 2 for close-in markets (depth bonus rewards composition), 4 for isolated markets (isolation already carries the score). T1.b scoring correction: tight clusters with ≥3 members score as T1. Civic (hospital/university) ×1.15. Country normalization (v0.4): raw scores are min-max normalized within each country before global ranking, so the best site in Greece competes on equal footing with the best site in Germany. Nordic countries (DK+SE+NO+FI) are normalized as one group. Underlying database: 6,493 clusters, 18 countries.

§6  ·  Research Context

Composition + Isolation: A Dual-Signal Index

Oxford Economics, CBRE, and Colliers International cover the largest 50–100 metros by GDP and population. This list does the opposite: it surfaces regional anchor towns — places that serve a wide catchment independently of any major metro. v0.3 scoring balances two signals: geographic isolation (how far a market sits from competing retail hubs) and tier composition (which anchor categories co-locate). Markets score well on either signal; the strongest markets score on both.

  • Discovery hubs lead: Fort Wayne IN #1 (isolated 107 km, multi-T1) and Esbjerg DK #1 EU (isolated 92 km) hold top positions — genuinely underserved regional catchments.
  • Established suburbs now score fairly: Sherwood Park AB enters at #57 — a T1 market 9 km from Edmonton that was suppressed by the prior isolation-only formula. Mesa AZ #4, Plano TX #6, Mississauga ON #7.
  • No fixed distance band: A market's class is set by anchor_d — distance to the nearest external T1 hub — derived from co-location data, not a curated metro radius.
  • T1.b correction: Compact clusters (tight, ≥3 members) that the taxonomy build mis-classifies as T2 are promoted to T1 at score time — closing a known build gap without a full rebuild.
